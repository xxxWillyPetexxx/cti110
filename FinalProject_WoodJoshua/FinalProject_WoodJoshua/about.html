About

Just wanted to take some time and tell you a little bit about myself.
Growing up, I was never really interested in computers. My grandfather was a computer
programmer and also built computers on the side, but other than the few times I went to his
house, I never really interacted with a computer outside of school. It wasn't until I was about
26 years old when I really started to learn how computers worked and became interested in computer gaming.
Right then and there I became hooked and entered into the world of computer building. My first gaming
I built was not very good. I did not do very good research before I purchased parts and really didn't invest
time in watching benchmarks, I just remember being so excited to finally build my first computer
Well, 7 years later and probably 2 - 3 dozen custom built computers under my belt, I finally feel comfident
enough to share my knowlegde and hopefully help a few people so they dont make the same mistakes I did
when I built my first computer.
