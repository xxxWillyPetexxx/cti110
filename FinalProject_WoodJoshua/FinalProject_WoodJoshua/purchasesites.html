PC Parts Websites

Below you'll find a list of my website recommendations for purchasing PC components.
This list is not exclusive and is just a few of the many retailers that you are able to
purchase PC parts from. I've just found that the below list typically offer some of the best deals
and from time to time and have to best combos (EX: Motherboard, Ram and CPU bundles) that can save you
the most money.

WEBSITE My Review
Newegg.com In my opionion, Newegg is an excellent site to utilize when building a PC.
Newegg offers many purchasing options from pay full price to financing and
overall is a pretty user friendly.
Amazon.com Amazon is a great website, especially if you are a prime member. Amazon also
consistantly offers deals on parts and depending on how you feel towards used
products, they have a decent used market also.
Ebay.com Okay, so Ebay can be a little tricky. While, I primarly recommend Ebay for those
who a looking for used products, you are able to catch good deals on new items also.
Be careful though, sometimes the deal is to good to be true and you might end up finding yourself
becoming a victim to a scam. Overall, if you vest the time in researching used product values and
making sure the seller is reputable, you can find yourself some really great deals
